Resume / Bio:




Nashville's Noelle Goodin is a dynamic singer, songwriter, and bandleader known for her soulful vocals, heartfelt lyrics, and commanding stage presence. With a sound that blends elements of folk, jazz, bluegrass, old time, and blues, Noelle crafts songs that are both deeply personal and universally resonant.

Drawing inspiration from traditional and contemporary influences, Noelle’s music captures raw emotion and storytelling, creating an intimate connection with her audience. Whether leading a full band or performing solo, her performances are infused with passion, authenticity, and a contagious and inviting energy.

As a band leader, Noelle brings together exceptional musicians from a variety of musical backgrounds to create a rich, immersive sound that elevates traditional repertoire as well as her original compositions. Her ability to seamlessly weave poetic lyricism with captivating melodies has earned her a dedicated following and recognition in the traditional and indie Americana music scenes.

Currently, Noelle is working on new music and performing at various venues and festivals, continuing to carve her path as a powerful voice in modern songwriting.
