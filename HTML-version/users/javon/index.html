Resume / Bio:

avon Everett, widely recognized by his stage name Visionz2turnt, is a multi-award-winning hip-hop artist, visionary performer, and actor whose creative impact is being felt across both the music and film industries. Known for his dynamic energy, compelling storytelling, and genre-blending artistry, Visionz2turnt has established himself as a unique voice in contemporary rap and Christian entertainment. With a career marked by authenticity and innovation, he continues to inspire audiences around the globe with his message, craft, and unwavering dedication to excellence.

 

Born with a passion for music and performing arts, Javon Everett discovered his creative voice at an early age. Raised in a culturally rich environment, he was exposed to diverse musical influences that ranged from gospel and soul to old-school hip-hop and modern trap. These early exposures laid the foundation for his own musical style—deeply emotive, lyrically profound, and rhythmically infectious. As Visionz2turnt, Everett channels real-life experiences, faith, and streetwise insight into music that resonates with listeners on a personal level.

 

His breakthrough came with the release of the powerful music video "Embers," a visual and sonic masterpiece that catapulted him into the spotlight. The video, acclaimed for its raw emotion, cinematic direction, and spiritual depth, earned a staggering 32 awards across international and domestic film and music festivals. The accolades ranged from Best Music Video, Best Cinematography, and Best Editing, to Audience Choice Awards. "Embers" has been praised not only for its artistic execution but also for its bold commentary on personal transformation, redemption, and resilience in the face of hardship. The project elevated Visionz2turnt from an underground sensation to a respected name in the industry.

 

Beyond music, Everett has also made his mark as a talented actor. His performance in a Christian short film, where he played the male lead, garnered widespread acclaim and earned him the Best Male Lead Actor Award at a prestigious faith-based film festival. In this role, Everett portrayed a character grappling with spiritual trials and personal redemption—a performance that was lauded for its emotional depth and authenticity. His ability to bring characters to life with vulnerability and strength has positioned him as a rising talent in faith-centered storytelling.

 

The name Visionz2turnt itself symbolizes Everett’s artistic mission: to turn up the intensity of one’s vision, purpose, and passion, especially in the face of adversity. His music often blends themes of struggle, hope, transformation, and triumph, making him a voice for the voiceless and a beacon for those navigating life’s darker chapters. Tracks like “Pathway,” “Chains Off,” and “Hearts on Fire” reflect his commitment to authenticity, while still pushing the sonic boundaries of contemporary hip-hop.

As a live performer, Visionz2turnt is known for electrifying shows that combine hard-hitting lyrics, high-energy delivery, and interactive moments that connect deeply with his audiences. Whether headlining regional festivals, youth conferences, or opening for nationally recognized artists, he brings the same passion and precision to every performance. His shows are often described as both invigorating and spiritually uplifting—an experience that leaves fans moved and motivated.

 

In addition to his music and film work, Everett is actively involved in community outreach and mentoring young artists. He regularly visits schools, churches, and youth centers to speak about overcoming adversity, pursuing dreams, and staying true to one’s faith and purpose. He often cites his own struggles and breakthroughs as testimony to the power of perseverance and divine guidance. His life and career serve as a living example of what can happen when talent meets purpose.

Everett is also a strategic thinker behind the scenes. He has played an integral role in the creative direction of his music videos, promotional campaigns, and brand development. Working closely with videographers, choreographers, and visual artists, he ensures that each project reflects his values, message, and artistic vision. This hands-on approach has allowed him to maintain a high level of authenticity while also appealing to a broad and diverse audience.

 

His discography continues to grow, with several EPs and singles that showcase his lyrical prowess and sonic versatility. He is currently working on his highly anticipated full-length album, which promises to blend hard-hitting tracks with introspective ballads and gospel-infused anthems. Each new release further cements Visionz2turnt’s place in a growing movement of artists who are reshaping the landscape of inspirational hip-hop.

 

Industry peers, critics, and fans alike recognize him not just for his accolades, but for his integrity and impact. The combination of artistry, faith, and social consciousness makes him a standout in today’s crowded music scene. With each project, Javon Everett continues to build a legacy that transcends entertainment—using music and film as platforms for healing, empowerment, and truth.

 

Looking ahead, Visionz2turnt shows no signs of slowing down. With new collaborations on the horizon, continued engagement in faith-based media, and an ever-expanding fanbase, his journey is only just beginning. Whether on screen, on stage, or behind the mic, Javon Everett remains committed to his calling: to ignite hearts, shift perspectives, and turn visions into reality.

 

In a world often driven by trends and superficiality, Visionz2turnt is a breath of fresh air—an artist who refuses to compromise on message, meaning, or mission. His work speaks to the soul, challenges the mind, and energizes the spirit. With each verse and every performance, he proves that real vision is more than what you see—it's what you believe, pursue, and live out loud.
