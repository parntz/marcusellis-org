I play bass and tuba for touring broadway musicals. I also have been the orchestral librarian, and since 2008 I've been the 'band tech', responsible for getting everything set up, from the truck to the pit, and after the performance, packed up and back to the truck. These additional duties have given me tech, and management skills.
